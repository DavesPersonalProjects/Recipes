Cookies!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Butter
  • Eggs

How to Cook

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees farenheit
  2. Melt butter
  3. Add ingredients in bowl
  4. Stir with spoon
  5. Put dough on ungreased pan
  6. Place in oven for 1 hour
  7. Cookies are ready!
