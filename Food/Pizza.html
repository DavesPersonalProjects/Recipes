Pizza Time!

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Peppers
  • Tomato Sauce

How to Cook

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees farenheit
  2. Roll your dough into a circle
  3. Add the tomato sauce on
  4. Add Cheese on top
  5. Add Peppers and Pepperoni on top
  6. Put Pizza in oven
  7. Take out after 30 minutes
  8. Pizza Time!
